About Us


This project "Rent On Click" is made to provide an online facility of getting some kind of places on lease on 'Hourly Format'. As its name suggests, one can take and Get places on rent just by a click...!!

Here, just by registering themselves, people can upload the pictures and details of the places they have which are unused. Summing up this, One can become tenant or squire with a little effort of click.
Rent On Click - a solution for earning and saving.



FEATURES


1. One can easily take any place for pre-availed slot of time with highly secured and hygiene freak environment.
2. One can allot their empty places on fixed amount keeping limited number of constraints.
3. Non-registered user too can see the available places.
4. Searching with filters is also possible.
5. Cancelling is an option before verification.
6. Online payment eliminates traveling charges and cash handling responsibility. (Go green promoter)
7. Query and suggestions can be fired to the website which will be replied and received by the manager directly within 24 hours.
